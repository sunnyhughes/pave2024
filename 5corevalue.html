Our 5 Core Values

PAVE embraces 5 core values in our strategy development and implementation that include:

  1. Innovation: We deliver innovative approaches and programming in parenting support and interventions.
  2. Supportive: We provide a supportive, caring, and judgement-free environment that facilitates and encourages healthy and stable families.
  3. Responsibility: We create a positive and engaging environment where parents can learn and grow.
  4. Accountability: We encourage accountability, transparency, and openness among the leadership team, parents, and the parent/child relationships.
  5. Intentional:We work to increase our understanding of the needs of parents and their children and to be strategic in developing relationships to empower parents.
